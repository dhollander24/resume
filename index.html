About Me

Aspiring criminologist and data analyst with a multidisciplinary foundation in Criminology, Applied Data Analytics, and Business, complemented by a certification in Forensic Social Work. I’m especially passionate about leveraging data analytics to inform policy decisions that foster stability and sustainable development globally.
